模块一 语言知识模块

1. pursue
释义：to do something or try to achieve something over a period of time ; to continue to discuss, find out about or be involved in something. to follow or chase somebody/something, especially in order to catch them.
You will learn to get by on very little sleep, meet fascinating people, and pursue new passions.

2. enthusiasm
释义：a strong feeling of excitement and interest in something and a desire to become involved in it. something that you are very interested in and spend a lot of time doing.
I want to encourage you to make the most of this unique experience, and to use your energy and enthusiasm to reap the benefits of this opportunity.

3. opportunity
释义：a time when a particular situation makes it possible to do or achieve something.
I want to encourage you to make the most of this unique experience, and to use your energy and enthusiasm to reap the benefits of this opportunity.

4. responsibility
释义：a duty to deal with or take care of somebody/something, so that you may be blamed if something goes wrong. a duty to help or take care of somebody because of your job, position, etc.
We believe that as you become members of our community of scholars, you will soon come to recognize that with the abundant opportunities for self-enrichment provided by the university, there also come responsibilities.

5. inheritor
释义：a person who is affected by the work, ideas, etc. of people who lived before them. a person who receives money, property, etc. from somebody when they die.
You are the inheritors of the hard work of your families and the hard work of many countless others who came before you.

6. inquisitive
释义：asking too many questions and trying to find out about what other people are doing, etc. very interested in learning about many different things.
I encourage you to stay inquisitive, but remember this: "Education is what remains after one has forgotten everything he learned in school."

7. embrace
释义：to put your arms around somebody as a sign of love or friendship. to accept an idea, a proposal, a set of beliefs, etc., especially when it is done with enthusiasm.
Embrace it as a challenge.

8. assured
释义：confident in yourself and your abilities. certain to get something.
Be assured that one day, you will find your own meaningful career, and you will connect a beautiful curve through those dots.

9. blossom
释义：a flower or a mass of flowers, especially on a fruit tree or bush.
You will form bonds of friendship that will blossom over many decades.

10. genuine
释义：real; exactly what it appears to be; not artificial. sincere and honest; that can be trusted.
You are a genuine and sincere person.

11. bold
释义：(of people or behavior) brave and confident; not afraid to say what you feel or to take risks. (of shape, color, lines, etc.) that can be easily seen; having a strong clear appearance. (technical) (of printed words or letters) in a thick, dark type.
Be bold!
